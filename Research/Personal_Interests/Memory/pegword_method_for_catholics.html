Option One

  1. 1 - Sun: The sun gives light representing the light of the Son of God. John 8:12: "Again therefore, Jesus spoke to them, saying: I am the light of the world: he that followeth me, walketh not in darkness, but shall have the light of life."
  2. 2 - Shoe: Imagine Jesus with his sandals. John 1:27: "The same is he that shall come after me, who is preferred before me: the latchet of whose shoe I am not worthy to loose."
  3. 3 - Tree: Imagine the tree of knowledge of good and evil.
  4. 4 - Door: Imagine the a door and seeing the four evangelists walking towards Christ.
  5. 5 - Hive: Imagine St. Ambrose around a beehive eating raw, organic honey with five bees flying around the hive.
  6. 6 - Sticks: Imagine the branches that will become the crown of thorns.
  7. 7 - Heaven: Imagine all of the saints in Heaven. The seven sacrament provide us with graces.
  8. 8 - Mate: Imagine two friends (mates) discussing the eight beatitudes.
  9. 9 - Wine: Imagine the water that was turned into wine at the wedding feast at Cana.
  10. 10 - Hen: Imagine a hen laying ten eggs which each represent one of the ten commandments.

  4. 4 - Floor: Imagine Teresa of Avila kissing the ground (chapter 34 of her Autobiography

  7. 7 - Leaven: Imagine our Lady making bread from yeast for Jesus and Joseph.
  8. 8 - Gate: Imagine St. Peter standing at the gate of Heaven with the keys to bind and loose.
  9. 9 - Vine: Imagine a bright vine representing Christ, the true vine (John 15).
  10. 10 - Pen: Imagine St. Jerome making the Vulgate. Book of Psalms 44:2: "My heart hath uttered a good word: I speak my works to the king; My tongue is the pen of a scrivener that writeth swiftly."
