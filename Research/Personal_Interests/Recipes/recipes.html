Coffee in a French Press

  1. Grind 15 grams of beans for every 250 grams of coffee
  2. Pour hot water into french press with properly grinded beans
  3. After four minutes, stir the coffee and removing any floating bits at the top
  4. After five minutes, bring the plunger down until it touches the top of the coffee (as though it were floating)
  5. Drink the coffee
